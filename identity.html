Workwear & Decal Notes

Embroidery: Use wordmark only (BAY CURRENT / DIVING) in Syne 800. Logo mark works as patch on chest-left. Signal orange on deep navy or charcoal.

Boat hull decal: White on dark, high-contrast. Logo mark + BAY CURRENT DIVING + baycurrentdiving.com + phone. One line. No clutter.

Truck decal: Full rear window — large BAY CURRENT DIVING in Syne, phone number in DM Mono below. White on dark tint.

Workwear palette: Charcoal coveralls, navy Carhartt work shirts, black waders. Signal orange patch/stripe as accent. No loud graphics.
